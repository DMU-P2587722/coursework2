THE STORY SO FAR...

The Mandalorian is set 7 years after the fall of the Empire. We follow a lone-wolf Bounty Hunter who goes by the name of The Mandalorian.
With the Empire fractured into remnant groups, the galaxy is still under threat from the Imperial terror. When the Mandalorian is sent off to track down a valuable bounty, he didn't expect his life to change.
Starring Pedro Pascal, Carl Weathers, Gina Carano and many more, The Mandalorian is an action packed adventure that both new and old Star Wars fans will enjoy.
With Jon Faverau, Dave Filoni and Taika Waititi working behind the camera, you wouldn't want to miss this cinematic masterpiece.
