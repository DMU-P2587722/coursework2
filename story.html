S T O R Y

CHAPTER 1: THE MANDALORIAN

A lone-wolf bounty hunter, called The Mandalorian, is given a contract that will change his life forever. Having been tasked with retrieving an asset, he ventures to Arvala-7 to track the mysterious bounty. When there, he meets an Ugnaught called Quil. They travel to an abandoned mining town, where other hunters are at work. Teaming up with the droid assassin IG-11, they work together to reach the asset...




CHAPTER 2: THE CHILD

After accquiring the asset, which happened to be The Child, Mando kills IG-11 so that The Child isn't killed. Soon, he heads back to his ship. Along the way, greedy bounty hunters ambush him and try to claim the bounty. Once the hunters were dealt with, Mando returns to find his ship dismantled by the local Jawas. He chases after them but has no luck. As a compromise, he is tasked with killing the Mudhorn, a rhino-like creature. When fighting the Mudhorn, he is almost bested, however, The Child uses the force to stop the Mudhorn in its tracks...




CHAPTER 3: THE SIN

Once the Razor Crest has been restored, Mando and The Child return to Nevarro. With a bad feeling and suspiscion, Mando hands over The Child to the Imperial remnant and is rewarded with a crate of pure Beskar Steel. Taking his reward to the underground Mandalorian sect, Mando is forged a suit of pure Beskar. But was it worth it?




CHAPTER 4: SANCTUARY

After escaping Nevarro, The Child and Mando arrive on Sorgan to escape the bounty hunters chasing them. They run into a Rebel Shock Trooper by the name of Cara Dune. Dune, Mando and The Child arrive at a small village that has recently been attacked by bandits. As a promise to the villagers, Mando and Cara help fight off the bandits. But does it go to plan?




CHAPTER 5: THE GUNSLINGER

After a bounty hunter tracks Mando to Sorgan, Mando decides to make a stop at Mos Eisley. Here, he meets a young bounty hunter wanting to make his name. After talking with the wannabe, Mando agrees to help him with taking out Fennec Shand. They track her down to the dunes outside Mos Eisely and hold out. A fight ensues and Fennec is captured. When she talks to the wannabe, she lets something very important slip...




CHAPTER 6: THE PRISONER

To earn some money along the way, Mando accepts a contract to break a high value target out of prison. When he meets his team, he finds that a Twylek from his past is working with him. Mando, Migs Mayfield and the crew board the New Republic prison ship, however, things don't go as planned...




CHAPTER 7: THE RECKONING

After his close run in with the New Republic, Mando travels to Nevarro after recieving a transmission for Greef Karga. Tempted, Mando arrives and meets with him. Greef Karga tells him of the plan that the Imperial Remnant have hatched. Mando assembles his team ready to take down the Client and his small number of forces, however, it seems Moff Gideon has other ideas...




CHAPTER 8: REDEMPTION

Mando, Cara, IG-11 and Greef are surrounded by Moff Gideon's forces. With no escape, the crew shoot their way out and make their way to the Mandalorian sect in the sewers. Here, the Armourer gives Mando a new jetpack and brands him with the seal of the Mudhorn. Together, the gang works together to take down Moff Gideon.




CHAPTER 9: THE MARSHAL

Mando returns... After many months, Mando has recieved news of a Mandalorian on Tatooine, in the village of Mos Pelgo. When he reaches the village, the local marshal Cob Vanth greets him. Donning an eroded set of Mandalorian armour, Cob tells Mando how he came to earn the suit. Then, the Crait Dragon attacks the village. Together, Mando, Cob and Tusken Raiders work together to taken down the dragon...




CHAPTER 10: THE PASSENGER

After the run-in with the Crait Dragon, Mando is ordered to take a passenger to the moon of Trask, where she can release the last offspring of her kind. Willing to take her, they set out. Out of nowhere, two X-wings stalk Mando and start shooting. In an attempt to run from the X-wings, Mando crash lands on a frozen planet. But there is something lurking in the ice caves...




CHAPTER 11: THE HEIRESS

Barely managing to reach Trask, Mando crash lands on the ocean moon. Here, he delivers the passenger to her husband and he goes to find the other Mandalorians. things turn sour as a group of Quarren fisherman attack Mando and The Child. Suddenly Bo-Katan and two other Mandalorians swoop down and rescue him. Mando agrees to return the favour and helps hijack an Imperial Freighter full of Mandalorian weapons...




CHAPTER 12: THE SIEGE

Making a quick pit stop at Nevarro, Mando meets with Cara and Greef. Nevarro has now become a colourful community thanks to Greef's supervision, however, there is still an Imperial presence. Mando agrees to help rid Nevarro of the Imperial Remnant on Nevarro for good...




CHAPTER 13: THE JEDI

Having been sent to Corvus by Bo-Katan, Mando is on the look for Ashoka Tano. She can help guide The Child to more of his kind. When he arrives, the local Imperial Warlord asks Mando to hunt Ashoka. Not giving in, Mando finds Ashoka but doesn't kill her. After a long discussion, The Child is discovered to be Grogu (a young Jedi from the Corruscant Jedi Temple). Together, Ashoka and Mando team up to take down the local warlord.




CHAPTER 14: THE TRAGEDY

After meeting Ashoka Tano, Mando takes Grogu to the seeing stone on Tython. Here, Grogu reaches out to the remaining Jedi through the force. This will help guide him to his people. However, things get complicated when Boba Fett and Fennec Shand turn up to claim Boba's armour...




CHAPTER 15: THE BELIEVER

Desparate times call for desparate measures. Mando has tracked down Migs Mayfield and needs him to infiltrate and steal the location of Moff Gideon. They pose as Imperial drivers and escort a highly volatile fuel in order to get the information. But things aren't as simple as they seem...




CHAPTER 16: THE RESCUE

With Grogu kept as a hostage on Moff Gideon's cruiser, Mando, Boba, Fennec, Cara and Bo Katan join forces to rescue Grogu and take down Moff Gideon for good...
