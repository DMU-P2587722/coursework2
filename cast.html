DYN JARRIN

B I O

Dyn Jarrin, AKA The Mandalorian, is a Mandalorian Bounty Hunter. With his trusty ship the Razor Crest, he flies round the galaxy and lives the nomadic lifestyle. When he is given a contract to track down an important asset, he didn't think it would change his life...


Dyn Jarrin is played by award winning actor Pedro Pascal.

CARA DUNE

B I O

Cara Dune was a Rebel Shock Trooper during the galactic civil war between the Empire and the Rebels. She served on Alderaan before it was blown up by the Death Star. After leaving the Rebels, she fled to Sorgan and lived in the bar, fighting people for money. After meeting Mando, she stayed with Greef Karga on Nevarro and served as the town marshal. However, she was offered to join the New Republic as she was a veteran of the wars. Now, she is a marshal in the New Republic.
